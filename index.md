Hello! My name is Colton, and I am a second-year Ph.D. Student in the Harvard/MIT Program in **[Speech and Hearing Bioscience and Technology (SHBT)](https://shbtphd.hms.harvard.edu/)**. I am advised by [Dr. Evelina Fedorenko](https://www.evlab.mit.edu/) and [Dr. Nancy Kanwisher](https://web.mit.edu/bcs/nklab/) in the **[Department of Brain and Cognitive Sciences (BCS)](https://bcs.mit.edu/)** at MIT. I am also generously supported by the **[Kempner Institute for the Study of Natural and Artificial Intelligence](https://kempnerinstitute.harvard.edu/)** at Harvard where I am a Graduate Fellow. 

I completed my undergraduate studies at Princeton University with a degree in [Neuroscience]() and minors in Computer Science and Machine Learning. I conducted my undergraduate research in the lab of [Dr. Uri Hasson](https://hassonlab.princeton.edu/), which first sparked my interest in using tools from machine learning to better understand language in the brain.
